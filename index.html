About

Cozy Crafts is a space to slow down and create
together. Whether you crochet, paint, journal,
or bring any project you love, our relaxed craft nights
are all about comfort and community. What started
as a group of friends completing IB CAS
creativity hours turned into a club, because the creativity
(and fun) didn’t deserve to stop.
